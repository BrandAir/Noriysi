About

Noriysi was created to reveal the invisible thread most migrants share in their story. It's a space to trace journeys, preserve stories, and understand where we've come from and where we're going. From first generations to children of migrants, from any continent, there are so many untold stories.

At its heart, Noriysi is about community. We have seen various diaspora groups connect and build their communities back up in a new land. This was part of the key inspiration behind Noriysi's existence. Picture a 3rd generation connecting with their culture from a first generation and relearning this culture and history.

Migration is not just movement — it's memory. By collecting individual stories and origins, Noriysi helps us piece together a fuller picture, discovering the why, the courage, and the heart of the people behind the journey.

Every profile, every story adds to a growing dataset that reveals patterns of movement, connection, and change. This isn't just information — it's insight that can shape how we understand diaspora communities

Story

On the 10th of march, I, my sister and my mom landed in Amsterdam. My dad and his friend waiting to pick us up. My dad's friend asked my mom "do you smoke?", with a confused look on her face she replies no. He said well you are about to become a smoker. As we stepped outside the airport, we indeed started smoking. I remember the scent in air, the chill and our breath becoming visible for the first time.

Generation Information

First Generation (1st Gen): Individuals who were born in another country and immigrated.

Generation 1.5 (1.5 Gen): Individuals who immigrated to a new country before or during their early teens.

Second Generation (2nd Gen): Individuals who were born in the country their parents immigrated to.

Third Generation (3rd Gen) & Beyond: Individuals whose grandparents or earlier ancestors immigrated.
